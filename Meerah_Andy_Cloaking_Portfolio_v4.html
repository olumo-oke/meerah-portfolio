Why It Matters

Your Campaign Dies
Without Protection

Ad platforms like Meta, Google, TikTok and Bing use automated bots and human reviewers to crawl every landing page before and after approval. For sensitive niches supplements, crypto, gambling, vapes, CBD, peptides, nutra the real offer gets flagged instantly.

Cloaking separates your real visitors from bots and reviewers. Real buyers see your actual offer. Crawlers and reviewers see a clean, compliant whitepage. Your account stays safe. Your campaign keeps running.

Without it: bans, disapprovals, wasted spend. With it: campaigns run for months without interruption.

🚫 Ad Rejection & Account Bans
Sensitive offers get flagged within hours of launch sometimes within minutes. Cloaking prevents the real page from ever being seen by platform reviewers.
💸 Wasted Budget on Bot Traffic
Without filtering, platforms send bot traffic, competitor clicks and policy crawlers to your page you pay for all of it. Cloaking blocks non-human traffic completely.
📉 No Free Test Available
Cloaking requires paid professional tools Keitaro, BeMob, custom whitepages. This isn't a service where a "free demo" exists. Quality setup protects a real business asset.
🔒 Long-Term Account Safety
A properly configured cloak with geo filters, device filters, referrer rules and ISP blacklists keeps ad accounts safe for months, not just days.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out. Still unsure? Use the chat widget at the bottom right.

What is ad cloaking and how does it work?+
Ad cloaking is a traffic filtering technique that shows different content to different visitors. Ad platform bots and reviewers see a clean policy-compliant page (the whitepage). Real human visitors get redirected to your actual offer page. This keeps your ad account safe while your real campaign runs freely.
Which countries do you work with?+
I work with clients from every country worldwide. Whether you are in the US, UK, Europe, Africa, Middle East, Southeast Asia, Australia or anywhere else, I can set up and manage your campaign. Geo targeting is one of the core filter layers I configure per setup.
Which ad platforms do you support?+
I support Meta Ads (Facebook and Instagram), Google Ads, TikTok Ads, Bing/Microsoft Ads and other demand-side platforms. Each platform has different bot fingerprints and review behaviours, and I configure filters specifically for each one.
What niches do you work with?+
I specialise in sensitive and restricted niches including adult supplements, crypto and forex, iGaming and gambling, weight loss, vape and e-cigarettes, cannabis and CBD, research peptides, nutraceuticals, and other offers that ad platforms flag or reject. If your niche is not listed, reach out and I will assess it.
How long does setup take?+
Standard setup is 3 to 4 days from payment of the first 50%. This includes whitepage build, tracker configuration, redirect rules, filter setup and testing from multiple IPs and devices. You receive a live walkthrough call before paying the final balance.
How does payment work?+
Payment is split: 50% upfront to begin work and 50% on delivery. Before you pay the final balance, we do a live screen share where I show you the full setup running in real time. You see the redirect working, the tracker dashboard and the ad account. You only pay the balance when you are satisfied.
Is there a free test or trial?+
No. Cloaking requires paid professional tools including licensed tracker software, dedicated VPS hosting, custom whitepage development and domain setup. These are real costs. What I offer instead is a live walkthrough before your final payment so you can verify everything is working before fully committing.
How long will my campaign stay alive?+
Results vary by niche and platform but my track record speaks for itself: longest active campaign is 8 months. I include 30 days of post-launch monitoring to adjust filters if platform behaviour changes. Most campaigns run for months when set up correctly.
What if my ad account gets banned after setup?+
During the 30 day monitoring window, if issues arise due to the cloaking setup I will investigate and fix at no extra charge. Bans caused by unrelated factors such as bad creatives, payment issues or prior account history are outside scope. I will still advise on next steps.
Do you provide the ad account or do I need my own?+
Both options are available. You can provide your own ad account or I can source one depending on your platform and niche. If you need a fresh account for Meta, Google or TikTok, let me know during the discovery discussion and I will factor that into the plan.
Can you cloak if I have already been banned?+
Yes. A ban on a previous account does not stop you from running a fresh setup. I build everything from scratch including new domain, new whitepage and a clean ad account where needed. The key is starting clean rather than trying to recover a flagged account.
